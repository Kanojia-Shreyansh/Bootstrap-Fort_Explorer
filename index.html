Golconda Fort

Golconda Fort, once the capital of the medieval Golconda Sultanate, is known for its brilliant acoustics and majestic structure. It is located in Hyderabad, Telangana, and features grand gates, drawbridges, and cannons from centuries past.

Mehrangarh Fort

Mehrangarh Fort in Jodhpur is one of the largest forts in India. It stands on a perpendicular cliff, four hundred feet above the skyline of Jodhpur. The fort includes palaces, galleries, and temples that reflect the richness of Rajasthani heritage.
